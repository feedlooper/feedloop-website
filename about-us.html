About FeedLoop

Building circular protein infrastructure for Canada.

FeedLoop is an integrated bio-manufacturing company focused on converting industrial pre-consumer organic waste into high-performance animal feed and organic soil inputs. Our model is designed around negative input costs, scalable automation, and resilient domestic supply chains.

By aligning gate-fee economics, automated bioconversion systems, and multi-output revenue streams, FeedLoop transforms waste liabilities into valuable protein and fertilizer products — strengthening food security while reducing landfill dependence.

Mission

To convert organic waste into valuable protein inputs while strengthening Canada's agricultural supply chains.

Approach

Automated Black Soldier Fly bioconversion, structured feedstock contracts, and disciplined infrastructure deployment.

Vision

To become Canada's largest integrated organic waste processor and animal feed producer by 2035.
