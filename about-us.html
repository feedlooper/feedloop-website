About FeedLoop

Building circular infrastructure for food systems

FeedLoop diverts industrial pre-consumer organic waste and converts it into high-performance animal nutrition and organic soil inputs. Our model is designed for repeatable supply, measurable diversion impact, and strong unit economics.

What we do

We partner with processors and farms to make diversion simple and reliable—turning organics into consistent protein meal, oil fractions, and frass fertilizer.

Why it matters

BC needs domestic, resilient feed inputs and lower-cost pathways for organics diversion. We help build that local supply chain while reducing landfill burden.
