What we do

1) Secure feedstock

We accept pre-consumer organics such as fruit & vegetable culls, brewery grains, and bakery waste.

2) Automated bio-conversion

We use automated EntoReactors to grow Black Soldier Fly larvae on optimized blends.

3) Multiple revenue outputs

Whole dried BSF, organic frass, further processing into protein meal & LipidX oil, plus tradable carbon credits.

Our vision

To become Canada’s largest integrated animal feed producer and organic waste processor by 2035, securing national food supply chains.

Why now

Industrial processors face rising disposal costs and increasing regulatory pressure on organics. FeedLoop turns that liability into a local protein and fertilizer supply engine.

Work with us
