Why FeedLoop

We’re building the missing link between waste producers and protein demand: a modern “loop” where organics become a valuable input, not a liability.

Problem

Organics are expensive to dispose

Industrial organic by-products often incur hauling, processing, and compliance costs. We make diversion simple and measurable.

Solution

Bioconversion at industrial scale

Our BSF system converts organics into protein meal, oils, and frass fertilizer with rapid cycle times and strong nutrient recovery.

Edge

Optimization + repeatability

We design for consistent yields, reliable uptime, and data-driven operations—so sites can scale like infrastructure, not farms.

What we produce

Protein meal

High-quality insect protein for aquaculture, pet nutrition, and specialty livestock applications.

Oils

Functional lipids (including lauric acid) that can support formulation performance and animal health outcomes.

Organic fertilizer

Frass-based soil amendment that returns nutrients to local agriculture—closing the nutrient loop.
