What we’re looking for

Feedstock (pre-consumer organics)

Fruit & vegetable culls, brewery grains, bakery waste, and similar consistent streams.

Buyers

Farms and producers seeking locally-produced protein inputs and organic soil amendments.

Pilot-minded partners

Partners who want to test, validate, and scale a long-term circular solution in BC.

We prioritize clean, consistent streams and long-term relationships. If you’re unsure, reach out anyway.

Two ways to partner

1) Supply organic waste

If you generate pre-consumer organics, we can evaluate your stream and logistics for intake. We aim to make diversion simple, measurable, and operationally reliable.

Helpful details: type of material, volume/week, contamination risk, pickup/dropoff, location.

2) Buy FeedLoop products

If you want to buy local insect protein or organic frass fertilizer, we can align on specs, volumes, and a trial pathway. We focus on consistency and repeatable supply.

Helpful details: application, desired specs, trial volume, timeline, delivery location.

3) Host a facility (optional)

If you have suitable industrial space, utilities access, and interest in hosting a modular unit, we can explore site requirements and partnership models.

Helpful details: address/region, utilities (power/water), zoning, access, available area.
