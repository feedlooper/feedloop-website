Meet the Team

Builders of circular protein infrastructure.

FeedLoop is led by engineers focused on scalable bioconversion systems, disciplined execution, and investor-grade unit economics.

Krit Unmole

Co-Founder | Chief Executive Officer
Krit holds a degree in Chemical Engineering and brings a long-standing commitment to sustainable infrastructure and circular bio-manufacturing. He has spent years researching and refining the FeedLoop concept, building technical insight and strategic relationships within the entomology and waste-conversion ecosystem.

He leads FeedLoop’s engineering strategy and commercial execution, focusing on scalable organic waste conversion systems, industrial automation, and thermal energy recovery. With experience managing multi-million-dollar capital projects, Krit drives the company from concept to industrial deployment.

Known for his infectious energy and ability to rally teams around a shared vision, Krit combines technical discipline with entrepreneurial drive to accelerate FeedLoop’s growth.

Adbhut Mangal

Co-Founder | Chief Technology Officer
Adbhut is a Professional Engineer (P.Eng) with a background in chemical and biomedical engineering and formal training in entrepreneurship. He has led multi-million-dollar capital projects and specializes in process optimization, systems integration, and yield maximization.

At FeedLoop, Adbhut oversees technology strategy, reactor performance optimization, and production efficiency, ensuring scalable and commercially viable bio-conversion operations. His project management expertise (PMP) supports disciplined execution from pilot to industrial scale.
