TECHNOLOGY

Modular BSF bioconversion designed for repeatable scaling.

We run a closed, controlled process that transforms pre-consumer organics into protein, oils, and fertilizer—optimized for uptime, consistency, and measurable diversion impact.

How it works

Step 1

Pre-process feedstock

We standardize moisture, particle size, and contamination thresholds so the biology stays stable and predictable.

Step 2

Larvae conversion

BSF larvae convert organics into biomass on short cycles, producing consistent protein and lipid output.

Step 3

Harvest & refine

Outputs are harvested and refined into dried meal, oils, and frass-based fertilizer for local markets.

What makes it scalable

Standard modules

Repeatable reactor “blocks” allow faster deployment across sites and predictable unit economics.

Data-driven operations

We track yield, cycle time, mortality, and feedstock variability to continuously improve performance and reduce cost per tonne.

What we optimize

Yield

Increase larvae output per tonne of feedstock through tighter process controls and recipe design.

Uptime

Reduce downtime with monitoring, preventative maintenance, and operational playbooks.

Quality

Maintain consistent specs (moisture, protein, fat) for buyers with QA and batch controls.
