Our Technology

Industrial bioconversion at scale.

FeedLoop deploys automated Black Soldier Fly (BSF) bioconversion systems to transform pre-consumer organic waste into high-performance protein and organic soil inputs.

Our model integrates structured feedstock contracts, automation, and energy-efficient process design to create scalable circular infrastructure.

Process Overview

1. Waste Intake

Consistent, clean pre-consumer organic streams are secured through long-term supply agreements.

2. Bioconversion

BSF larvae convert organics into high-protein biomass through rapid growth cycles.

3. Processing & Output

Outputs include insect protein meal, oil fractions, and organic frass fertilizer.

Why Our Model Works

Negative Input Economics

Gate-fee aligned waste streams create a cost-advantaged input structure, improving margin resilience.

Automated Infrastructure

Process automation reduces labor intensity and increases consistency across modular facility deployments.

Multi-Revenue Outputs

Protein meal, oil, and frass fertilizer diversify revenue streams and reduce single-market exposure.

Energy Efficiency

Integrated thermal management and process flow optimization improve system-level performance.

Built for Canadian Agriculture

FeedLoop’s systems are designed for integration into existing agricultural and food processing ecosystems — strengthening domestic feed resilience while reducing landfill dependence.
